Abilities:

Q: laser gun

Hold down Q for a continuous fire laser with a base fire rate of 10 shots per second. Hitting an enemy with this ability heals you for 0.2 health.

W: homing bombs

Press W to fire 6 homing smart bombs with targeting system that ensures unique target selection per bomb. 10 Second Cooldown

E: rapid fire mode

Press E to increase the attackspeed of your twin lasers to 100 shots per second and deal 2x damage to bosses. 20 Second Cooldown

R: sword and shield

Press R to gain a shield that absorbs all incoming damage for 10 seconds. 20 Second Cooldown Press R while the shield is active to fire a wide laser that deals damage that scales with the damage the shield absorbs squared 1 minute cooldown.

Movement:

Hold down Arrow Keys to move.

Rounds

Each round you will face a wave of enemies that shoot at you. When you kill all the enemies in the wave, another wave will spawn. Every 3 rounds, you will face a boss that you must kill. The higher the round, the more difficult the enemies. There are an infinite number of rounds in this game, so you can play until you die.

Health

For each small enemy you kill you gain 0.2 health and for each boss kill you gain 5 health. Your maximum health starts off at 20 and increases by 1 at the start of each boss round. When hit by an enemy projectile, your health will decrease until it reaches 0,then it's game over.

Score

Killing a small unit grants 100 points and killing a boss grants 1000 points. The points you earn are multiplied by your score multiplier, which can be increased by killing small enemies and bosses. Small kills increase your score multiplier by 1 and boss kills increase it by the round number squared. When you get hit by a projectile, your score multiplier is quartered until it reaches 0.
